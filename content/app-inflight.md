# In-flight Food Ordering App

**An app to help passengers order food inside a plane.**

## Problem Statement

Help flight passengers place in-flight food orders for immediate consumption through a mobile app.

## Understanding the Goal

Travelling by air is one of the most popular means of transport. Food is a crucial part of the journey. Making this experience delightful would leave a lasting impact on passengers.

An aeroplane carries people from various cultures, languages, and regions, with different abilities, disabilities, and dietary restrictions. The challenge for cabin crew is to cater to every single passenger with the exact meal they expect.

### Why This Matters

- Reduce congestion on aisles
- Avoid awkward conversations between passengers and flight attendants
- Analyze eating patterns to save money and decrease food waste
- Current in-flight catering global market size: $8.5B
- 1.14 million tonnes of food wasted from in-flight catering in 2017

## Defining the Audience

- **Geography:** Entire spectrum across the globe — requires internationalization
- **Culture:** People from different faiths and cultures with specific eating preferences
- **Medical conditions:** People needing dietary restrictions for various conditions
- **Age:** Entire spectrum: 1 year to 80 years

## Understanding Context & Customer Needs

When passengers use the app, they are already onboard and up in the sky. Currently, ordering food is a hassle — call cabin crew, place order, anxiously wait hoping the order is right. An app provides a much better experience for ordering quickly without assistance.

## Solution — User Story

Ravi, a 25-year-old software developer, travels from Hyderabad to Singapore:

1. Downloads the in-flight app before boarding
2. Opens app and enters email for sign up
3. Answers simple questions about dietary preferences
4. Selects meal and snack types from preferences
5. Homepage with menu opens
6. Selects food and continues to checkout
7. Pays through online transaction and places order
8. Cabin crew gets notification with order details, preferences, and seat number
9. Crew prepares food and delivers
10. Ravi uses "assistance" button for help with cutlery
11. Great experience with food and service
