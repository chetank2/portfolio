# We Wrk — UI Design

**Connecting people with artisans for home repairs.**

## Description

People are busy and often don't get time for home repairs. Finding a reliable repair person is difficult. Questions arise: How to find a good artisan? How much do they charge? How to contact them? How to keep track of the work?

Artisans also face difficulty finding work in their locality unless someone recommends them.

This application helps people and artisans connect and get repairs done.

## Style Guide

- Primary: #0077FF
- Background: #F7F7F7
- Text: #001833
- Secondary: #7E7F81
- Border: #D4D4D4
- Typography: Poppins (32px → 12px scale)

## Key Screens — Customer Side

### Explore Screen
Finding artisans in nearby locations based on location and type of help. After selecting artisans, users can connect directly by chatting and checking artisan profiles.

### Work Screens
Shows ongoing, completed works and schedule screen where users can schedule works and reminders.

### Cancelling Work
Users can cancel work if not satisfactory and submit feedback directly.

### Profile & Notifications
Check & edit profile. View notifications about feedback, completed works, and reminders.

## Key Screens — Artisan Side

### Sign Up Screens
Artisans sign up with phone number, update security card details along with job snaps to show clients.

### Explore Screen
Artisans explore work opportunities through map, find users by distance, and chat.

### My Jobs Screen
Requested, ongoing & completed jobs in one place. Profile screen showing artisan details and previous work snaps.
