# Learn Hadiya

**A user-friendly web app for foreigners to easily learn the Hadiya language in Ethiopia.**

**Live:** [learnhadiya.com](https://www.learnhadiya.com/)

## Overview

An educational platform dedicated to teaching Hadiya, a language spoken in Ethiopia. Hosted by Wachemo University and designed by Konic Technologies.

## Target Audience

- Those with no Hadiya knowledge
- Non-speakers wanting to learn
- Those seeking fluency improvement
- Individuals aiming for multilingual proficiency

## Course Structure — "Hadiyyisa in 10 Days"

- **Part 1:** Contextual phrases
- **Part 2:** Structural phrases with grammatical formulas and rules
- **Part 3:** Contextual vocabulary

## Teaching Methodology

Rather than traditional translation-based approaches, the course uses "logical language learning formulas with stipulated rules, examples, oral and written drills." Enables learners to construct sentences independently and apply them across various real-world situations.

## Content Coverage

Present, past, future, continuous present, and continuous past tenses through dialogue-based scenarios presented in clear table formats.

## Navigation

Home, Gallery, Learning modules, Team information, with a prominent "Start Learning" call-to-action.
