# Fit X

**A fitness app designed for tracking goals, team collaboration and creating personalized plans.**

## The Problem

Many individuals face challenges in maintaining regular physical activity and achieving their fitness goals, especially when relying on gym memberships or fitness programs. A common issue is the lack of motivation, leading to incomplete programs or frequent gym absences. Additionally, existing apps often fall short, providing non-customizable workout plans that fail to meet users' specific needs.

## The Goal

Create a mobile app that addresses these issues by offering a user-friendly platform for tracking daily physical activity and promoting team collaboration to enhance motivation. The app aims to provide customizable workout plans, empowering users to tailor their fitness routines according to their preferences and track their activities effectively.

## My Approach

- **User Motivation** — Implement features that foster team collaboration and support to boost user motivation
- **Customizable Workouts** — Develop a user-friendly interface allowing individuals to create personalized workout plans
- **Activity Tracking** — Provide real-time feedback and visualizations to enhance the user experience and progress awareness
- **User Collaboration** — Implement challenges and group activities to promote a sense of community and accountability

## Design Process

Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test

### Empathize

**Goal:** Know about users and find patterns in their fitness lifestyle and form a strategy.

- **User Interviews** — Potential user interviews and surveys helped identify the problems they face with current apps
- **Affinity Mapping** — Gathered all answers from users and used affinity mapping to structure the data. Color codes make it easy to follow answers from different questions. Categories and tags support classification of results.

### Key Pain Points from Research

- Lack of active tracking
- Lack of plan makers
- Lack of group coordination
- Too many ads
- Frequent notifications

### Persona

**Ashok**, 30-year-old professional with a busy schedule, making it challenging to consistently attend gym sessions. Despite initial enthusiasm for fitness programs, he often lacks the motivation to complete them. Values the sense of community and accountability that comes with collaborative fitness experiences.

**Goals:**
- Consistent physical activity to improve overall well-being
- Personalized fitness journey with customizable workout plans
- Community connection and support to enhance motivation

**Challenges:**
- Motivation hurdles when working out alone
- Limited customization in existing fitness apps
- Misses the sense of community from group fitness activities

### Define — User Stories

1. As a fitness enthusiast, I want to track my daily activity so I can monitor progress and stay accountable
2. As a user, I want to create customized activities to tailor workout plans to my preferences
3. As a social fitness enthusiast, I want collaborative workouts with friends for mutual motivation

### Ideate — Competitive Analysis

Analyzed Cultfit, Nike Running, and Adidas Running apps. Identified gaps in active tracking, plan creation, and group coordination.

**How Might We:**
- Design a feature that encourages users to actively track physical activity throughout the day?
- Enable users to create personalized workout plans aligned with their goals?
- Design features that promote friendly competition and collaboration among users?

## Solution

Based on research, designed three core features:
1. Showing regular stats and progress of workouts
2. Making users build & customize their workouts
3. Making users create teams with friends and create team workouts

### Information Architecture

Feed, Nutrition, Social → Stats, Workouts, Teams → Individual & Team goals

### Style Guide

- Primary: #002A4B
