# To Do — Reimagine App

**Reimagining the to-do app for work-from-home employees.**

## Problem

In this century, everyone is so busy they often forget small household tasks and crucial work meetings. Life is about balancing work and personal life — focusing too much on one ruins the balance.

People use generic to-do lists and reminders, but: **Do generic to-do lists work for the current era of people working from home?**

## Target Users

Employees working from home — even though office timings are clear, personal and work life always conflict. They need to attend to household chores between meetings and work.

## Understanding User Behaviour

Talked with 5 users about working from home and managing tasks. Key pain points:

1. **Fragmented accounts** — Work and personal tasks in different accounts or linked to different emails. Can't see both in one app. Hard to find conflicting tasks.
2. **Cross-platform issues** — Some apps don't work cross-platform or don't sync to multiple devices.
3. **Anxiety triggers** — Many apps show number of tasks left, creating anxiety when tasks pile up.
4. **Limited widgets** — Existing apps provide simple read-only widgets. Users need to open app to do anything.
5. **Tedious input** — Adding new tasks requires lots of inputs. Most apps don't integrate voice assistants (Siri, Google Assistant, Alexa).
6. **Annoying recurring tasks** — Low-priority recurring personal tasks like "get a haircut every 25 days" are always annoyingly visible and hard to postpone.
7. **Poor integration** — Don't integrate tasks from different tools and calendars like Google Calendar and Microsoft Outlook.
8. **Hard rescheduling** — Rescheduling missed tasks requires lots of input again.

## Redefined Problem Statement

Work-from-home employees find it difficult to manage day-to-day personal and work tasks. They might forget urgent personal tasks during work meetings or miss work tasks due to personal ones. This mismanagement causes frustration.

## Reimagined Features

1. **Unified view** — Show all tasks from work and personal accounts in one place with clear distinction
2. **Voice & NLP integration** — Integration with voice assistants and natural language processing to reduce time adding new tasks
3. **Cross-device sync** — App supports and syncs with all devices (computers, laptops, mobile)
4. **"Free time" metric** — Show "free time in a day" instead of incomplete task count to reduce anxiety
5. **Smart postponing** — Intelligently postpone low-priority recurring tasks based on user's schedule
6. **Useful widgets** — "What's next", "free time for the day" and more
7. **Family sharing** — Share tasks with family members so they know when you're busy with meetings
